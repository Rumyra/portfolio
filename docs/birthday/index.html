What

It's both an afternoon garden party and an evening shindig! Drop by when you want and stay for as long as you want!

Garden Party from 2pm

Chill out with some pimms and snacks. Don't worry if you've got little ones - loads of guests do! They'll be party food and there's playing fields and a play area right next to the hall.

Evening Shindig 6pm onwards

Bring your own! There'll be evening food and some provided drinks, but bring with you what you need. There's no license to sell alcohol but you can bring whatever you want.
